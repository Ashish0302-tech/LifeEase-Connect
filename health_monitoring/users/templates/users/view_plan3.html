Plan for Achieving Normal BMI

Steps:

  • Regular exercise: Engage in at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity most days of the week.
  • Healthy eating: Consume a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to maintain hydration levels.

Precautions:

  • Avoid crash diets: Aim for gradual, sustainable weight loss rather than rapid weight loss methods.
  • Consult a healthcare professional: Before starting any new exercise or diet plan, consult with a doctor or nutritionist.

Diet Plan:

Here is a sample diet plan for achieving and maintaining a normal BMI:

  • Breakfast: Whole grain cereal with low-fat milk and sliced fruit.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, vegetables, and vinaigrette dressing.
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with berries.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with steamed vegetables and quinoa.

Remember to customize your diet plan according to your individual preferences and nutritional needs.
