Diet Plan for Men (65+)

Steps:

  • Balanced Diet: Focus on a balanced diet consisting of a variety of nutrient-dense foods.
  • Portion Control: Be mindful of portion sizes to maintain a healthy weight and prevent overeating.

Diet Plan:

  • Breakfast: Whole grain cereal with low-fat milk, topped with fresh berries.
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Greek yogurt with chopped nuts and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Diet Plan for Women (65+)

Steps:

  • Nutrient-Dense Foods: Emphasize nutrient-dense foods to meet changing nutritional needs.
  • Fiber-Rich Diet: Include plenty of fiber-rich foods to support digestive health and manage cholesterol levels.

Diet Plan:

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with sliced bananas and walnuts, served with a glass of fortified orange juice.
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus.
